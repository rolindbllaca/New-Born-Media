We're looking for a UI/UX Designer who will be responsible for collecting and translating user insights into meaningful experiences. Ultimately, the designer will be expected to create both functional and appealing features that address the users' needs. As a UX/UI designer the person we're looking for will work with other designers, product managers and development teams to together create and deliver strategy, information architecture, UI mockups, prototypes, design specs, MVPs, and final products.


Responsibilities:


  • Gather and evaluate user requirements in collaboration with product managers and engineers;
  • Illustrate ideas using storyboards, process flows, info architecture maps, wireframes, and prototypes;
  • Design graphic user interface elements;
  • Develop UI mockups and prototypes;
  • Develop UI mockups and prototypes;
  • Prepare and present rough drafts to internal teams and key stakeholders;
  • Identify and troubleshoot UX problems;
  • Create and adhere to Design specs and guidelines.
